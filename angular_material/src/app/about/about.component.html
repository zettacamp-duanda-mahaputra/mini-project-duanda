Waroeng Mbak Atik

LOCATED ON A VIBRANT PEDESTRIANISED MARKET STREET, WAROENG MBAK ATIK IS A BRIGHT AND AIRY SPACE WITH GLAZED FRONTAGE ON TWO SIDES AND AN OUTSIDE TERRACE ON THE MARKET. SERVING OUR SIGNATURE WELL-TRAVELLED BREAKFAST AND ALL DAY FOOD AND DRINKS MENUS, ALONG WITH OUR FRESHLY ROASTED COFFEE FROM THE CARAVAN ROASTERY, OUR STYLE IS OPEN, RELAXED AND FRIENDLY. COME DOWN FOR A COFFEE WITH THE MORNING PAPER, A COCKTAIL AND SMALL PLATES IN THE EVENING AND ANYTHING IN BETWEEN!
